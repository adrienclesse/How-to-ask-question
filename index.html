Guidlines : how to ask a question

General

  • make sure your grammar and spelling is correct..
  • Always stay polite and communicate with empathy
  • Be structured and acurate

Title

  • Write your title after having written your question.
  • Include the programming language and the program (if relevant)
  • Should be well summarized and to the point.

Introduction

  • Need to be clear what you are doing before you ask the question
  • Say what you have already done/tried
  • Explain what didn’t worked
  • Don’t ask questions impulsively, structure your ideas and give yourself a deadline to look for answers before asking it.
  • Google your question first and see if there isn’t already a similar problem online with a solution that you can try.

Technical

  • Include the steps of how you encountered the issue, how other people can reproduce your issue.
  • If you get an error, include that too, and if necessary, link a screenshot.
  • Include a sample of your code if you think it’s relevant. Don’t add unnecessary code though.
  • If it’s relevant, mention the urgency of your question. Sometimes you need a quick solution.
